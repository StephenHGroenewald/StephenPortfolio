Skydiving and experiencing the ultimate rush of free fall is a high-adrenaline activity that I absolutely love. Funnily enough, I actually have a fear of heights—I get a little scared just climbing onto a roof! Yet, somehow, I love flying planes and jumping out of them. Stepping out of an airplane high above the ground and plunging through the air is the ultimate masterclass in conquering that fear and enjoying pure, unadulterated freedom. 😂
