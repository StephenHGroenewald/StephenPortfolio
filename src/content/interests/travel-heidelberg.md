Visiting historic European cities like Heidelberg and relaxing at charming outdoor cafes is a travel experience I love. Sitting under a red umbrella, sipping a drink, and taking in the historic brick walls and green trees is the perfect way to soak in the relaxed European atmosphere. Beyond just enjoying the view, I have a deep passion for photography and, even more importantly, videography. I really enjoy capturing the essence of these beautiful places on camera and editing the videos together to bring the whole experience to life.
