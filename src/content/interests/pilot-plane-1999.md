I was never actually interested in aviation, but my best friend and business partner Ross, generously paid for me to do a flying course with him. I am incredibly grateful he did, because taking control of the aircraft and navigating the open skies turned out to be one of the most liberating and exhilarating experiences of my life. Being up in the clouds offers a unique perspective on the world and a thrilling sense of freedom, made even better by sharing such a beautiful and unique journey with him and his brother Mark.
