My introduction to skiing was back in 1999 on a trip to Cortina, Italy, and it remains an experience I absolutely cherish. The slopes were beautiful, but the absolute highlight was that the Bobsleigh World Championships were happening right then. We actually got to ride down the official bobsleigh track as passengers behind actual world-class drivers. Rocketing down that ice track is still, to this date, the most amazing and unforgettable adrenaline rush I have ever experienced.
